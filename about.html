About the Project

ChronoMap is an interactive historical exploration tool that allows you to travel through time and space, discovering significant events that shaped our world. From ancient civilizations to modern achievements, explore history in a unique and engaging way.

Data Sources

Our historical data is carefully curated from reliable sources including:

  • Wikipedia
  • DBpedia
  • Academic historical databases
  • Verified historical records

Technology Stack

  • Mapping: Leaflet.js with OpenStreetMap
  • Timeline: noUiSlider
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Contribute

Help us expand our historical database! If you'd like to submit a historical event, please ensure you include:

  • Event title and date
  • Precise location (latitude/longitude)
  • Category and description
  • Reliable source links
